About Gen AI

Get Started Easily And Learn About Gen AI

Generative artificial intelligence refers to AI systems that can produce text, images, videos, or other types of data by leveraging generative models. These models learn the patterns and structures within their training data and can then create new content that mirrors those characteristics, typically in response to specific prompts.

Face Recognition and generate

Face recognition is a type of computer vision that uses optical input to identify or group people in images or videos. It works by comparing the faces in two or more images to determine the likelihood of a match. Face recognition systems use artificial intelligence (AI) and deep neural networks to analyze images and create facial signatures.
